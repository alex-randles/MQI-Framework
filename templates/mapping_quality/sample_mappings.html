Personnel Mapping

This mapping uses the Friend of a Friend (FOAF) Ontology to transform the name (NAME), friends (KNOWS) and location (LOCATION). This mapping contains 3 quality issues:

  • Incorrect Term Type.A subject map cannot have a term type (rr:termType) of a literal (rr:Literal).
  • Usage of undefined class. foaf:Persons is undefined in FOAF.
  • Usage of undefined property. foaf:knows is undefined in FOAF.
  • Usage of incorrect range. foaf:based_near requires a range (rr:termType) of an object (rr:IRI) rather than a literal (rr:Literal).
